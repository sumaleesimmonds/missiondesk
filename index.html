PEOPLE
Small organizations deserve the same tools as large ones.

A two-person nonprofit and a Fortune 500 foundation compete for the same grants. Only one has a development team. MissionDesk closes that gap.

Built by Sumalee Simmonds, a self-taught AI builder from a mixed-heritage, first-generation immigrant family. Her mother's family came to this country as Laotian refugees. She builds AI tools because technology should reach the people who have historically had to fight hardest just to be seen.

Built in alignment with Pacific Community Ventures and the International Rescue Committee. Powered by Anthropic's Claude API.
